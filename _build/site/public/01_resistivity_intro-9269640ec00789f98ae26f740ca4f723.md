# Introduction to Electrical Resistivity Tomography (ERT)


- ERT allows to map subsurface properties in 2,3D and time
- ERT is sensitive to subsoil electrical conductivity/resistivity changes 
- ERT has been applied to explore in many context: civil enginerring, ecohydrology, forest ecology, ...
